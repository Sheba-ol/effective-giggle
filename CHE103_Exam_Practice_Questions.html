📋 Quick Reference
n
n = m ÷ M (moles = mass ÷ molar mass)
M
Molarity = mol ÷ Volume(L)
C₁V₁
C₁V₁ = C₂V₂ (dilution formula)
pH
pH = −log[H⁺]
Rf
Rf = dist(spot) ÷ dist(solvent front)
PV
P₁V₁ = P₂V₂ (Boyle's Law)
V/T
V₁/T₁ = V₂/T₂ (Charles' Law)

6.022 × 10²³ mol⁻¹

E° = 0.00 V (reference electrode)
